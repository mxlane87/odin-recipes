Tacos


Description

This recipes is about tacos. Tacos may be the greatest food ever invented.

Ingredients

  • Corn Tortillas
  • Ground Beef
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Seasoning
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Fry the gorund beef with the seasoning
  2. Cook the Tortillas till they're nice and warm
  3. Fill the tortillas with the beef, cheese, and salsa
  4. Enjoy!
