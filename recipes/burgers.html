Hamburgers


Description

This recipes is about hamburgers. Burgers are killer when it comes to hitting the spot.

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns
  • Ground Beef
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Mayo
  • Ketchup
  • Sliced Onions

Steps

  1. Shape the ground beef into patties, then fry on a grill or cast iron pan.
  2. Toast the buns
  3. Assemble the burger: buns, then beef, then cheese, onions and condiments.
  4. Enjoy!
